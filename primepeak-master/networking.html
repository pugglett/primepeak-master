How to setup a Home Network Video

Hiw to setup a Home Network Lesson

Topic:

Setting up a home network and the required device along with the necessary cables.

Why and How:

A computer home network is a very common thing as well as essential nowadays. It allows for more mobility and no longer are you confined to a small space where your computer is connected. Even Desktops can be setup in many different places provided that the computer is within the range of a network and fitted with the proper equipment. Setting up a computer network is very easy and this video we will show you how to do it. The video will follow a simple YouTube tutorial format which will last between a minute and 2. Bryce will be filming me as I explain the different components involved in setting up a computer home network.

Content:


 Brahulio will walk into the LCM and introduce himself as well as the topic that he will be discussing for the mod III project.
 Brahulio will talk about the modem and router, explaining how the modem receives the internet signal from the Internet Service Provider Company, while the router distributes that signal into a wireless form for all the devices to receive.
 Brahulio will connect DSL cable (Green) in the DSL labeled port of the modem while explaining that this connection grants the modem access to the internet, thus granting access to the user of modem.
 Brahulio will insert the Ethernet cable (Blue) into the ports labeled Ethernet behind both the modem and router. Brahulio will explain that it is this connection that allows Wi-Fi services to devices within the network range.
 Brahulio will then connect the power cords in both the devices and outlets to turn on the modem and router.
 To wrap things up, Brahulio will explain that a functioning modem has 4 solid green lights. However, since the modem in the tutorial does not have an Internet Service Provider, it has a red blinking light that indicates just that.

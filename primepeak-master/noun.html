Nouns Lesson

Topic:

Learning what Nouns are and how to identify them in a sentence. Knowing what nouns are and how to identify them within a sentence is essential in writing and understanding the English language. Instead of learning in a potentially “boring” classroom type of setting, we have used a cartoon presentation to help show the importance of using and identifying nouns correctly. This video will appeal primarily to those who are visual learners; however, if notes are taken while the lesson is being taught, this video’s examples may help those who prefer the kinesthetic technique as well.

Content:

 A cartoon presentation will be used to demonstrate the use of nouns and its definition.
 Noun types such as concrete, abstract, proper, and common nouns are explained with examples
 Identifiable music to our targeted audience will be included
